The Methodology

The R.E.A.D.™ Framework

Every First Read™ is structured through four deliberate review lenses — not a checklist, a philosophy.

R Reveal

We start by seeing what's actually there — the real story your resume is telling, versus the story you believe it is. Most documents bury the lead. We find it.

E Evaluate

Every section is assessed for strategic strength, positioning clarity, and competitive alignment. Not against a generic rubric — against your specific goals and the market you're entering.

A Align

Your experience is reframed as a coherent professional narrative. Decades of depth must read like a deliberate arc — not a chronology. We close the gap between what you've done and what it signals.

D Direct

You leave with a clear, specific action plan. What to revise. What to keep. What to amplify. What to remove. Direction — not suggestions. You asked for a strategist. This is where that shows.
